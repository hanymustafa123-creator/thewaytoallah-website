🎙 Speaking Task

Self-Introduction Dialogue (حِوَار التَّعَارُف)

Imagine you are meeting your teacher for the first time. Answer the following questions out loud in Arabic, using full sentences. Your teacher will listen and mark you on pronunciation, accuracy, and confidence.
YOUR TEACHER WILL ASK YOU:
  1. Q1 — What is your name?
    مَا اسْمُكَ؟
    Expected answer: اسْمِي مُصْطَفَى. — My name is Mostafa.
  2. Q2 — Where are you from?
    مِنْ أَيْنَ أَنْتَ؟
    Expected pattern: أنا مِنْ ___. — I am from ___.
  3. Q3 — What is your nationality?
    مَا جِنْسِيَّتُكَ؟
    Expected pattern: أنا ___ِيّ. — I am [nationality].
  4. Q4 — Point to your teacher and say "This is my teacher."
    هَذَا مُدَرِّسِي.  /  هَذِهِ مُدَرِّسَتِي.
    Use هَذَا for a male teacher, هَذِهِ for a female teacher.
MARKING CRITERIA (Teacher Use):
  • 1
    Pronunciation (4 marks) — Correct sounds, especially ع ، خ ، ح ، ق
  • 2
    Accuracy (4 marks) — Correct vocabulary and pronouns (أنا / أنتَ / هُوَ / هِيَ)
  • 3
    Fluency (2 marks) — Responds without excessive hesitation
