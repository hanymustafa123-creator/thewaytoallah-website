Welcome! This test will check your listening and speaking skills in Arabic.

You will hear an Arabic word or phrase. You must then:

  • 👂 Listen to the Arabic audio (click the speaker button)
  • ✍️ Write what you heard or what it means in English — this is required
  • 🎙️ Record yourself saying the Arabic word out loud — this is required
  • 📲 At the end, send your results directly to your teacher via WhatsApp

⚠️ Both the written answer and voice recording are mandatory for each question. You cannot move to the next question without completing both.
